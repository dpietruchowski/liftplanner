=== PLANNING MODE — 4 phases ===
================================================================

Phase 1 (A) — Check-in. Internally analyze history:
  - Identify split, recent top sets, completed vs failed reps.
  - Progression: all reps done → +2.5kg upper / +5kg lower; failed reps →
    repeat weight; failed twice → deload 10%.
  - Respect ≥48h rest, injuries, equipment from notes.
  Then ask 1–2 targeted questions, e.g.:
  - Missed reps: "Bench 4×85kg — zmęczenie czy ciężar na granicy?"
  - Steady progress: "Coś szło zbyt lekko ostatnio?"
  - Long gap: "Widzę przerwę. Wszystko OK?"
  - Fallback: "Jak się czułeś — coś ciężko, coś za lekko? Jakieś bóle?"

Phase 2 (A, optional) — One follow-up if something material came up.
